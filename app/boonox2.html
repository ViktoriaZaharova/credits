What is Credits Bonoox?

Bonoox is a cloud service for launching your business loyalty program.

The main feature of Bonoox is the combination of new functionalities implemented on the Credits blockchain. Merchant's loyalty points are tokens similar to coins or new money.

Blockchain gives unique features which can’t relise on standard programs.

You can allow or prohibit transferring points between customers. Bonoox is a platform that gives you the ability to implement any of your mechanics.

Additionally Bonoox gives basic features like programs without blockchain features, API, marketing instruments, promotional codes, coupons, promotions, etc.
